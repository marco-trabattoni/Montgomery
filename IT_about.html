Montgomery started in 2016 as a work intended to examine the recurring patterns in timeless design. With thousands of curated images, the color-coordinated visual stream of consciousness has become a global mainstay and reference for the design community.Since its inception, Montgomery has grown in the last years into a collaborative creative studio that aims to inspire through multiple channels; through fabrication of items with purpose and longevity, through the design and construction of meaningful physical environments, and through the continued curation of its digital platform. In addition to its own projects, Montgomery has worked in collaboration with numerous established artists and brands to extend its strong design ethics.Located in Milan Italy, the design studio is operated by founder.
